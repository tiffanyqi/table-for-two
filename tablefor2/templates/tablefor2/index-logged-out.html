What's Table for Two?

Table for two is a program where you are randomly matched with someone outside of your team to have a 1:1 coffee or lunch with- either in the Cafe or offsite, the choice is yours!

Just fill out your information and preferences, and select when you're generally available. Our algorithms will match you with another Mixpaneler once a month based on your availability, team, and tenure at Mixpanel. Each month, you'll meet with someone new!
If you choose to Google Hangout, you'll also be able to meet other Mixpanelers in a location other than your own.
