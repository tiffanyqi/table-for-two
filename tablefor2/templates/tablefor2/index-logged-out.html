What's Table for Two+?

Table for Two+ is a program where you are randomly matched with someone (or group!) outside your team or location to have lunch, coffee or online hangout - the choice is yours!

Just fill out your information and preferences, and select when you're generally available. Our algorithms will match you with another Mixpaneler once a month based on your availability, team, and tenure at Mixpanel. Each month, you'll meet with someone new! If you select a group match, fill out your lunch availability.
If you choose to video call, you'll also be able to meet other Mixpanelers in a location other than your own.
